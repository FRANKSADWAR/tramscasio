Our Story

Founded in January 2020 to offer transportation services with a difference in Kenya and beyond. It’s a locally owned company versed in various economic activities. From the beginning, the strength of the company has been its unmatched quality services at competitive price. The past one year at Tramcasio Enterprises limited has been dominated by technologically advanced innovations which have become industry standards. Today, the company has a leading and unparalleled experience in transportation of Manufacturing products, lease of Pickup trucks, Tippers and flatbeds, General Transport support; and in addition, we offer loose and containerized cargo services sourced from the best markets around. We also have a specialized team which is always ready to score big with out-of- the-box ideas. Tramcasio Enterprises limited has significantly invested in creating high-tech teams that are focused on customer values and satisfaction, professionalism, safety of goods, good customer service and safety standards. The focused approach of the company’s management and staff make it possible to exceed the demanding expectations of our customers and attain excellence in our service.

Our Vision

To be a distinguished firm with the latest technology that promotes professionalism and integrity in providing an excellent transportation service to our clients.

Our Mission

To provide higj quality transporation services that meets our clientele daily needs and maintain excellence in implementation and support: while continuing to invest in high quality staff and related services and also manage through controlled growth and building close strategic partnerships.
